My Story

Hi there! I'm Bala, 24, junior developer, living in Los Angeles. I moved to America almost 16 years ago with my family from South Africa. We came for the same reason everyone does: A chance at the American dream. I started working when I was 10 years old helping my dad run his small bussiness selling oil paintings at malls thorughout Southern California, and have kept working through highschool and college.

On my 20th birthday I moved out with my then girlfriend to Los Angeles where she had gotten accepted to UCLA and I started at Pasadena City College. We both took full time jobs and worked to support ourselves while going to school. I lived and worked for 3 years under the table without papers before marrying my girlfriend and getting my green card. I am now at University doing my senior year of computer science, and in pursuit of my first job in the industry.
